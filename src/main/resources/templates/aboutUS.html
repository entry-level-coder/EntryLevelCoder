About Us

Entry-Level Coder is a specialized job board designed to help recent computer science graduates, coding bootcamp graduates, and individuals who have made a career switch into software development find their dream jobs. Our platform focuses on job opportunities that require 0-2 years of software development or web development experience, ensuring a more targeted job search for entry-level candidates.

Our mission is to bridge the gap between jobseekers with limited experience and employers looking for talented, passionate, and dedicated entry-level software developers. By providing a platform that exclusively features entry-level positions, we aim to streamline the job search process and facilitate meaningful connections between jobseekers and employers.

We understand that the job market can be competitive and overwhelming for entry-level candidates, and we strive to make the job search experience more accessible, focused, and efficient for both jobseekers and employers. We believe that every talented individual deserves a chance to launch their career, and we're committed to helping make that happen.
